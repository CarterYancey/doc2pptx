# Fine-Tuning, Multiverses, and Boltzmann Brains

## The Multiverse and Boltzmann Brains

### The Multiverse Concept
- Suggests physical reality consists of many universes, each with different physical constants.
- Aims to provide a statistical explanation for the appearance of fine-tuning.

### The Boltzmann Brain Problem
- Quantum fluctuations in a multiverse could produce "Boltzmann Brains" (randomly formed intelligent observers).
- If BBs are more likely than standard observers, our belief in a stable, standard universe is undermined.
- This creates a "self-defeating" problem for the multiverse hypothesis.
